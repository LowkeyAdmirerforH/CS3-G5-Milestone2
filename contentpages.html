What is corruption?

Corruption is the abuse of power for personal gain, often involving dishonest actions. It can include bribery, fraud, embezzlement, and nepotism. This behavior undermines trust in institutions and can harm social and economic development. Corruption negatively impacts fairness, accountability, and the rule of law in society.
